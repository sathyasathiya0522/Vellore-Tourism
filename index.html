About


Vellore is a city in the state of Tamil Nadu, in southern India. It's known for 16th-century Vellore Fort, with its imposing granite walls and surrounding moat. Inside the fort, the Jalakandeswarar Temple features many ornate sculptures. Nearby, the Government Museum has prehistoric relics and natural history displays. By the Palar River, the Muthu Mandapam memorial houses the tomb of a Tamil leader.Vellore, the Head- Quarters of Vellore District situated in 12′ 35′ N and 79′ 9′ E, has a very interesting History of its own. A strategically located town, it is well connected by Rail and bus routes to major towns of the neighboring states like A.P. Karnataka and Kerala. The History of the district assumes a great significance and relevance, as we unfold the glorious past. It was under the sway of various dynasties and rulers, the prominent among them being the Pallavas, Cholas, Rashtrakuta dynasty of Malkhed, Sambuvarayar, the rulers of Vijayanagara,Nawabs of the carnatic and the British. The Monuments found in the district give a vivid picture of the town through the ages. HOW TO REACH VELLORE

By Road


Tamil Nadu state buses, both government and private, run on a regular interval from the major southern cities like Bengaluru, Tirupati, Chennai, Pondicherry, and Tiruchirapalli. These buses, through the road network, give you the access to Vellore. However, one can also choose to travel by their own vehicle through roads and national highways, which connect these cities with Vellore. The National Highways connecting Vellore with other important cities are NH 40, 48, 77, and 38.

By Air


The nearest airport from Vellore is the Tirupati International Airport which connects the place with other major cities of the country through daily frequent flights. One can cover rest of the distance, which is just 113 km, by boarding a cab/taxi from the airport to Vellore. Tamil Nadu state buses are also available from the airport to the city. Airports at Chennai and Puducherry are also quite close to Vellore.

By Rail


Vellore has its own railway station which gives a hassle-free way to reach Vellore from major cities like, Mumbai, Chennai, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru along with other important cities of India.
